2.1.2 Scrolling Layout

For small number of vertically stacked charts, the charts will automatically fill the height of the browser by default. However, if there are too many vertically stacked charts, you can choose to either re-size them all and fill the page by using vertical_layout: fill or layout charts at their natural height and enable scrolling if necessary with vertical_layout: scroll.

---
title: "Chart Stack (Scrolling)"
output:
  flexdashboard::flex_dashboard:
    vertical_layout: scroll
---
